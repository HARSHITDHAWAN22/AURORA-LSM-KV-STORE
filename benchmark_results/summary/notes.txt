========================================
FINAL CONCLUSION
========================================

- Leveling performs better at smaller scale
- Tiering scales better as workload increases
- Bloom filters significantly reduce unnecessary SSTable reads
- Cache hit rate decreases with larger working sets, as expected
- AuroraKV shows realistic LSM-tree performance tradeoffs
